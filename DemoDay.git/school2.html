Bronx High School of Science stands as a pinnacle of academic excellence with a distinguished focus on STEM education. Its competitive admissions process ensures a student body teeming with talented individuals who excel in these fields. Graduates often secure placements in prestigious universities and contribute significantly to scientific research and innovation. Beyond academics, Bronx Science encourages participation in diverse extracurricular activities, fostering well-rounded individuals who succeed in both their passions and pursuits.

High School of American Studies at Lehman College

With a dedication to nurturing inquisitive minds, the High School of American Studies at Lehman College offers a unique educational experience centered around American history, government, and political science. This specialized curriculum empowers students to delve deep into the nation's rich historical tapestry while cultivating analytical thinking, critical reading, and effective communication. The school's affiliation with Lehman College provides access to a college environment, encouraging a seamless transition to higher education. Through engaging classes, research opportunities, and a close-knit community, the school prepares students to become informed and active citizens who contribute meaningfully to their society.
