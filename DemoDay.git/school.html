Stuyvesant High School

Stuyvesant High School, situated in Battery Park City, is renowned for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence and STEM education. Through a rigorous curriculum, competitive admissions process, and diverse extracurricular opportunities, Stuyvesant cultivates a community of high-achieving students. Its robust science, technology, engineering, and mathematics programs propel graduates into prestigious universities and impactful careers. With a history of producing accomplished individuals in various fields, Stuyvesant stands as a beacon of intellectual growth and achievement.

Eleanor Roosevelt High School

Eleanor Roosevelt High School, a beacon of education in the arts and humanities, empowers students to explore their creativity while maintaining academic rigor. By blending traditional subjects with artistic expression, the school fosters critical thinking, artistic prowess, and a well-rounded education. Located in Manhattan, Eleanor Roosevelt's commitment to nurturing both intellect and creativity enriches the educational journey for its students.

Beacon High School

Beacon High School redefines education through its progressive approach to learning. Emphasizing project-based learning and student engagement, the school prepares students to be active participants in their education. Located in the heart of Manhattan, Beacon offers a vibrant arts program, fostering creativity alongside critical thinking. With a focus on inquiry and exploration, Beacon equips students to tackle complex issues and thrive in an ever-changing world.

Millennium High School

Millennium High School, nestled in the bustling city, is dedicated to cultivating global citizens and critical thinkers. Through a curriculum centered around global studies and interdisciplinary learning, students gain a comprehensive understanding of the world's complexities. Millennium encourages students to engage with global issues, promoting informed decision-making and social responsibility.

Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts

Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School, commonly known as LaGuardia Arts, stands as a premier institution for aspiring artists, musicians, dancers, and actors. Situated in the cultural hub of Manhattan, the school offers a comprehensive education in the performing and visual arts. Through rigorous training and creative exploration, LaGuardia Arts empowers students to pursue their passions while fostering artistic excellence.

New Explorations into Science, Technology and Math High School (NEST+m)

New Explorations into Science, Technology and Math High School (NEST+m) provides a nurturing environment for accelerated learning in STEM subjects. With an accelerated curriculum and opportunities for advanced study, NEST+m equips students with the tools needed to excel in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Located in Manhattan, the school fosters a community of curious minds, preparing students to tackle challenges and pursue careers in STEM-related fields.
